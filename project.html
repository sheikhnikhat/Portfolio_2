Recently I am working with my college classmates on Fees Management System Website.

Description about Project :

A fees management system is designed to streamline and automate the fee collection process for educational institutions. This system efficiently handles no dues management, ensuring that all pending fees are tracked and managed, and reminders are sent to parents and students for timely payments. It also facilitates fees submission by allowing payments through various online methods such as debit/credit cards, net banking, and digital wallets, making the process convenient for users. Additionally, the system automatically generates and sends e-receipts to parents and students upon successful fee submission, ensuring a paperless and efficient process. These features collectively reduce administrative burden, improve financial management, and enhance communication between schools and parents.
